# Privacy Policy for Chief of Staff AI

Last updated: July 12, 2026

At Chief of Staff AI, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how your information is handled when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Access
Our application requests access to your Gmail and Google Calendar data to provide core features, including:
* **Gmail**: Reading and triaging your inbox to generate summaries and email drafts.
* **Calendar**: Accessing event information to facilitate meeting scheduling.

## 2. Data Usage
We only use your data to perform the specific tasks you request within the application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## 3. Data Storage
We do not store your email contents or calendar details on our servers. All processing happens in real-time. Authentication tokens are handled securely and are only used to authorize requests you initiate.

## 4. User Control
You can revoke our access to your Google account data at any time via your Google Account Security settings.
